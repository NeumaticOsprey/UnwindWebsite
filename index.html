About the idea


Unwind is a mental wellbeing app. It provides a safe place where users can relieve their anxieties to a helpful, understanding, non-judgemental ear.

At its heart is a chatbot powered by the IBM Watson API. Unwind recommends coping strategies based on users concerns. Unlike mindfullness apps, Unwind knows meditation isnâ€™t the answer to everything.

Design Process

For users to really value Unwind, it needed to speak to them in an understanding way. To ensure this, the team first created user personas to frame the delivery of Unwind.

Everything from sketches, to wireframes, high-fidelity designs, branding and tone of voice were validated against these personas.




The Prototype


The final prototype followed three user journeys: onboarding, and two specific use cases. As well as this the final prototype was an opportunity for Unwind to learn about human emotion and relevant responses to user concerns.
